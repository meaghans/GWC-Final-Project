The Importance of Mental Health


Maintaining your mental health is extremely important in high school. It can be difficult to set time for yourself when you are taking rigorous courses and balancing various extracurriculars, but time manangement is key! Always make sure to take care of your physical and emotional health. You come first, don't forget it!
