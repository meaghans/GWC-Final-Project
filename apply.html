Application Season

At first glance applying to college can seem like a scary thing, but in actuality it really isn't. The application process is only the first step that gets you through the door of the college experience.The key is to start early, be consistent, be careful, and alert your school. A key piece of advice when applying is to find a way to stand out. someof those ways include: building your academics, getting a handle on your tests, thinking outside of your school's extracirriculrs, and considering your recommendations. Most colleges will use the following two application portals. The first portal is Common App and the second portal is Coalition.

Important Documents

    Rec Letters

    Ask the person's permission

    Ask early(6wks-1month prior)

    Make sure you know them well

    Provide description of what you're apply for

    Provide the due date

    Follow up with the recommender

    Resumes

    Emphasize your education(GPA, class rank, ect...)

    Include extracirriculars & Volunteering

    Emphasize leadership experience

    List your skills(Computer, language, etc...)

    List Achievements

    List informal Work experience
