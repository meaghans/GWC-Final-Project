What to Expect

Congrats, you are oficially a Junior in high school. This will be one of the most difficult years in high school. Your workload will increase, you will begin taking standardized tests, and you will begin your college search early. The key is to not procrastine, get to know your guidance counselors, and not get to overwhelmed.
