College Readiness

Junior year is a very crucial year to most when it comes to high school. This is because 11th grade is the year where you begin to look into what colleges you want to attend, and begin writing your entrance essays during the summer. It is good to get a head start and begin to build a college list as well as look into what the qualifications to attend the colleges of your choice. This process can be very tedious; however, it will benefit you in the future.


College Prep Tips (Summer)


    College Specific

    Attend College Fairs

    Visit Colleges on Tour

    Prepare your College List

    Apply for FAFSA/Check Date

    Check College Application Dates

    Download College Applications/Start Common & Coalition

    Non-College Specific

    Register for the PSAT

    Make an SAT & ACT Schedule

    Register at NCAA Eligibility Center (Athletes)

    Fine Tune Extracirriculars

    Find a Full-Time or Part-Time Job

    Participate in Summer Camp or Summer Immersion Program
