High School in a Nutshell

The Effective Recap


The High School experience is all about involvement and time management. There are many things to remember. In 9th grade, you need to remember to be yourself, join clubs and sports, and maintain good grades. In 10th grade, you need to remember to prepare for the SAT and ACT, keep involvement in clubs and sports, take advanced classes and incorporate time management. In 11th grade, you need to remember to study for the SAT and ACT, plan for recommendation letters, apply for scholarships and summer programs, finalize community hours, and start college essays. In 12th grade, you need to remember to apply for college and scholarships by their respective deadlines. Also, make sure to have fun after 4 years of hard work!
