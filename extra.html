Spending Time on Activities


Extracurriculars and activities take up a large part of your high school experience.

Not only are extracurriculars important for college admissions, but they can also bring forth valuable experience in leadership, collaboration, and initiative. Therefore, extracurriculars, both in-school and out of school, should absolutely be a part of every experience in high school.
