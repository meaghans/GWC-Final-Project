What to Expect:


10th grade is difficult, but that's okay! Now is your time to shine. This page offers a plethora of resources, knowledge, and information for all Sophomores to use to their advantage.

Remember to work hard because when it comes to school, you get what you put in.

Keep your head held high. You're halfway through the journey. Now, get studying!

Advice:


"Precommit to getting 8 hours of sleep a night. It's tempting to stay up late because you have work to do, but then you get in a cycle where you can't work efficiently because you're too tired, but you can't go to sleep because you have too much work to do." - Jessica Su

"Begin looking for scholarships. A lot of people wait until senior year but you will be happy that you started early." - Austin Mitchell
