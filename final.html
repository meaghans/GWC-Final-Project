Our Inspiration

What made us create this website?

As high school students, we understand that high school is a stressful time that would be made easier if there was someone to guide or provide support.

High school can prove to be a hard time, whether it is caused by stress, too many classes, peer pressure, or extracurriculars. Occasionally, all high schoolers need is some guidance from people they don't personally know and this website is simply a guide to survive and thrive in high school.


The Purpose of This Website

What will this guide do?

This website is essentially a guide for high schoolers, starting from 9th all the way to 12th grade.

We decided to create this website to provide advice for students in order to help them leave high school with success and satisfaction. We also aim to inform students of important tests, events, and learning resources in accordance to every year.
